Supplies

Essential Tools and Materials

  1. Scissors
  2. Glue
  3. Tape
  4. Writing utensil of some kind
  5. A Notebook
  6. Memorabilia or photos of your choosing

Non-Essential Tools and Materials

  1. Stickers
  2. Stamps
  3. Decrotive Tapes
  4. Decrotive Papers, clippings, doilies, or magazine cut outs
  5. Markers
  6. Paint or other art supplies
  7. Ribbon or lace
  8. Beads or decrotive charms

Frequently Asked Questions

What supplies does one really need to life journal?
A pen, a notebook, and anything else you wish to paste in.
What is the difference between scrapbooking, journaling, and life journaling?
Life journaling is a mix of scrapbooking and journaling combined.
Who can life journal?
Everyone! If you can write or paste things into a book you can life journal.

Where can I get the supplies to life journal?

Anywhere that sells paper goods, craft supplies, office supplies, or a simple pen and notebook. Personally I reccomend places to get supplies such as your local Hobby Lobby or Amazon.
